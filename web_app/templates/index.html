Project Overview

Hospital readmissions are a major healthcare challenge, leading to increased costs and burdening medical facilities. This project aims to predict patient readmission risk using machine learning and statistical modeling, based on medical history, demographics, and clinical parameters.

By analyzing patterns in patient data, hospitals can implement preventive measures, optimize treatment plans, and reduce unnecessary readmissions.

Key Findings:

  • Overall readmission rate is 46.09%
  • Patients over 70 have 47.69% readmission rate
  • Patients with 9+ diagnoses have over 50% readmission rate
  • Longer hospital stays correlate with higher readmission risk
